How this works?

OUR PART                                                   YOUR PART

  • RFP Submission
    Simply submit a request for proposal (RFP) telling us the key areas where your company require help in and we will look into it!
  • Requirement elicitation
    Upon recieving your RFP, we will carry out the necessary research, come up with solution and prepare a pitch.
  • Initial meeting and Pitch
    Set aside some time to discuss the details of the project and hear how our solution will be beneficial for you.
  • Reiteration & changes
    Based on the information received during the meeting, we will make the necessary changes and send you the final proposal.
  • Acceptance of project
    Once the proposal is satisfactory, send the go signal for us to begin providing our services.
  • Execution
    Our team will either work remotely or at your office where necessary to complete the required tasks at satisfactory standards.
  • Feedback
    Your feedback on the quality and timeliness on our services are welcomed at any time throughout the project.
  • Completion
    Marks the end of the project period!
